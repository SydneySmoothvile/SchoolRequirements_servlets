WHAT WE DO?

We provide marginalized schools all over Kenya with a platform to show the help they need so as to provide quality education to their students, most of whom live in marginalized areas.

How previous donations have helped

Donations have helped schools across areas with pastoral communities build dormitories
enabling students learn effectively

Building domitories have enabled students attend all academic curriculums on time creating focus and little distraction

Schools have also managed to get electricity through donation of solar panels
